This calendar is shared: everyone who opens the page sees and edits the same schedule, and engineers are saved for reuse each week. Red weekdays and yellow Saturdays are not yet covered; green days are set. The ★ primary defaults to whoever signs up first — use Balance primary to spread that role evenly across the month. An engineer can right-click (or long-press) a day to claim primary, which locks it (marked with a lock icon) so balancing leaves it alone. Export CSV saves a readable snapshot of the month. Important: saved data is tied to the published version, so before re-publishing an updated calendar, click Backup to download everything, then Restore that file on the new version to bring the schedule back.
