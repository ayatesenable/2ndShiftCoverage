This calendar is shared — everyone sees and edits the same schedule, which lives in the database and persists across deploys. Every Mon–Fri is auto-assigned a ★ primary and a backup, balanced evenly. Click a weekday to toggle whether you can't cover it; the whole month (today onward) reshuffles automatically to fill the gap. Right-click or long-press any day to lock yourself in as primary or backup — locked claims survive reshuffles (shown with a lock icon). Weekends aren't auto-assigned. Saturdays are an open sign-up — click one to add the hours you can work; any number of people can sign up and overlapping hours are fine. Sundays show as no-coverage by default (we don't normally run then) — click one to claim it if a shift comes up. Right-click a weekday or Saturday and choose No coverage needed to grey it out for holidays or shutdowns — it's skipped by balancing until you restore it. Past days stay frozen as a record. Backup downloads a full snapshot and Restore brings one back — handy before big changes. Coverage rules lets you guarantee an engineer a set number of ★ primary days each week (spread out, back-to-back allowed only for them, never on days they've marked off).
